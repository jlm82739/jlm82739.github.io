iPhone VS Android

One of the most common arguments about modern technology is whether an iPhone or Android is a better device. In the end, it really comes down to personal preference. However, there are some key differences between the two that are vital to your decision.

One critical feature is the battery life of the device. In the past, iPhones were notorious for having a short battery life, especially after extensive use. The current iPhones are a vast improvement over iPhones of the past, however Android offers such a variety of devices that the battery life of some of their phones massively exceeds that of any iPhone. According to newatlas.com, Apple's devices go toe-to-toe with the competition, thanks to an operating system that's been optimized for efficiency.

Another important feature to compare is the interface. While iPhones are based more on simplicity and being user-friendly, Androids offer a very wide range of capabilities. Because of this, those who are more tech-savvy tend to prefer Androids because they are not as limiting.
